My Journey

From Engineer to Innovator

It began in 2011 as a Senior Electrical Engineer at Petrosilah. I saw inefficiencies and envisioned change—proposing a study that saved $420,000 annually. That moment sparked a 14-year mission.

Leading with Purpose

By 2018, as Head of Energy Efficiency at EGPC, I secured a $200M EU-EBRD loan, transforming Suez Oil Processing—saving 295,000 tons of CO2 yearly. It was about building a sustainable legacy.

Empowering Others
